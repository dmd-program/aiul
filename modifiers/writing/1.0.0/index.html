Writing v1.0.0

Overview

The Writing domain covers all text-based content creation, including essays, articles, reports, creative writing, technical documentation, and other written deliverables.

Scope

This modifier applies to projects and assignments involving:

  • Academic essays and research papers
  • Creative writing and storytelling
  • Technical documentation and guides
  • Blog posts and articles
  • Emails and correspondence
  • Copywriting and marketing content
  • Code comments and documentation
  • Poetry and literary work

Applicable AI Tools

When declaring AI usage in Writing, consider these tool categories:

  • Language Models - ChatGPT, Claude, and similar general-purpose AI assistants
  • Writing Assistants - Grammarly, ProWritingAid, and stylistic tools
  • Specialized Tools - AI for poetry, screenwriting, or specific genres
  • Content Generators - Tools that generate text from prompts or outlines
  • Grammar & Spell Checkers - AI-powered proofreading
  • Translation Tools - AI language translation
  • Summarization Tools - AI that condenses or summarizes content

Considerations

  • Writing is one of the most common domains for AI assistance
  • The boundary between Ideation, Assistance, Generation, and Refinement can be subtle in writing
  • Consider the assignmentâ€™s learning objectives when choosing your usage level
  • Even with substantial AI assistance, careful curation and editing can add significant value

Related Usage Levels

Pair this domain with a usage level to create a full declaration:

  • Ideation & Brainstorming - AI for outlines and brainstorming
  • Assistance & Debugging - AI for fixing grammar or suggesting improvements
  • Content Generation - AI for writing substantial content
  • Refinement & Polish - AI for editing and polishing
