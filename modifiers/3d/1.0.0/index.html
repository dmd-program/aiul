3D Design v1.0.0

Overview

The 3D Design domain covers work involving three-dimensional modeling, animation, virtual environments, and related spatial design activities.

Scope

This modifier applies to assignments and projects involving:

  • 3D modeling and sculpting
  • Character design and animation
  • Game design and virtual reality
  • Architectural visualization
  • Product visualization
  • Procedural geometry
  • Texture mapping and material creation
  • Motion capture and animation
  • Rendering and lighting design

Applicable AI Tools

When declaring AI usage in 3D Design, consider these tool categories:

  • Text-to-3D Model Generators - Generate 3D models from text prompts
  • AI-Assisted Modeling Tools - Interactive 3D modeling with AI suggestions
  • Automated Rigging Systems - Skeleton and bone automation
  • Motion Capture Assistance - AI-enhanced animation and motion data
  • Texture & Material Generators - Procedural or AI-generated surfaces
  • Environment Creation Tools - Procedural landscape and space generation
  • Rendering & Lighting AI - Automated lighting optimization

Considerations

  • 3D work often involves technical challenges where AI assistance can be valuable for overcoming blockers
  • Consider whether understanding of 3D principles or final output quality is the primary learning objective
  • Complex projects may require different AI usage levels for different components (e.g., modeling vs. rigging)
  • Some aspects like UV mapping or optimization might benefit from AI assistance even when other elements are restricted

Related Usage Levels

Pair this domain with a usage level to create a full declaration:

  • Ideation & Brainstorming - AI for concept exploration before modeling
  • Assistance & Debugging - AI to solve technical problems in your 3D work
  • Content Generation - AI for substantial model or asset creation
  • Refinement & Polish - AI for optimization, texturing, or final rendering
