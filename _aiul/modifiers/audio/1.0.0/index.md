# Audio v1.0.0

## Overview

The **Audio** domain covers sound design, music production, audio editing, voice work, and all audio-related content creation.

## Scope

- Music composition and production
- Sound effects and foley creation
- Voice acting and narration
- Audio editing and mixing
- Podcast and audio content production
- Sonic branding and audio design
- Speech synthesis and voice modulation

## Applicable AI Tools

- Music generation and composition tools
- Audio synthesis and effect processors
- Voice cloning and modification tools
- Audio upscaling and enhancement
- Automated mixing and mastering tools
- Noise reduction and cleanup tools
- Music theory and composition assistants
