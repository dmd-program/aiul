# Video v1.0.0

## Overview

The **Video** domain covers motion design, videography, animation, and all video-based content creation and post-production.

## Scope

- Animation and motion graphics
- Live-action videography and filming
- Video editing and post-production
- VFX and visual effects
- Compositing and color grading
- Livestreaming and real-time video
- Documentary and narrative video
- Video advertising and promotional content

## Applicable AI Tools

- AI animation generators
- Motion capture and performance capture tools
- Video upscaling and enhancement
- Automated editing and scene detection
- Color grading and correction assistants
- Background removal and object segmentation
- Synthetic actor and deepfake tools
- Real-time rendering with AI
